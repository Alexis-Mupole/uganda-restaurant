About Uganda Restaurant

Bringing the authentic tastes of Uganda to your table since 2018

Our Beginning

uganda RESTAURANT was founded in 2018 by Grace Namara, a passionate cook from Lira who grew up learning traditional recipes from her grandmother. Her dream was to share Uganda's rich culinary heritage with the world, starting with Kampala.

What started as a small eatery with just 20 seats has grown into one of Kampala's most beloved restaurants, welcoming over 25,000 guests who come to experience authentic Ugandan flavors in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Today, under the guidance of Head Chef Robert Sseguya, we continue to honor traditional recipes while adding modern twists. Chef Robert brings 15 years of experience in East African cuisine, having worked in renowned kitchens across Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda before joining our family.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Robert Sseguya

"Food is our love language. When we cook with passion, our guests taste it in every bite."

Chef Robert Sseguya has been crafting exceptional Ugandan cuisine for over 15 years. Born in Masaka, he began his culinary journey at a young age, helping his mother prepare family meals. His talent was spotted early, leading him to train at the Uganda Hotel and Tourism Institute.

After gaining experience in prestigious kitchens across East Africa, Chef Robert joined uganda RESTAURANT in 2019. His signature style blends traditional Ugandan cooking techniques with contemporary presentation, creating dishes that honor our heritage while exciting the modern palate.
