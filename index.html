Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Taste

Founded in 2018 by Grace Namara, uganda RESTAURANT was born from a passion to share Uganda's rich culinary heritage with the world. Growing up learning traditional recipes from her grandmother in Lira, Grace dreamed of bringing authentic flavors to Kampala.

Today, under the guidance of Head Chef Robert Sseguya, we continue this tradition. With 15 years of experience in East African cuisine, Chef Robert has mastered the art of balancing traditional flavors with modern presentation.
