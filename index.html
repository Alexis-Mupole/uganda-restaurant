Our Story

Taste the Spirit of Uganda

Founded in 2018, uganda RESTAURANT was born from a passion to share Uganda's rich culinary heritage with the world. Our founder, Grace Namara, grew up learning traditional recipes from her grandmother in Lira.

Today, under the guidance of Head Chef Robert Sseguya, we continue this tradition. With 15 years of experience in East African cuisine, Chef Robert has mastered the art of balancing traditional flavors with modern presentation.

We source our ingredients from local farmers across Uganda - from the fresh fish of Lake Victoria to the matooke from Mount Elgon region.
